Eilat: A City of Endless Sun

Eilat is a beautiful city located on the southern tip of Israel, known for its stunning beaches, coral reefs, and vibrant nightlife.

Recommended Attractions

The Underwater Observatory Marine Park is a must-visit attraction, featuring an incredible array of marine life and stunning coral reefs.

The Dolphin Reef is another popular attraction, where visitors can swim and interact with friendly dolphins in their natural habitat.

The Timna Park is a unique attraction, featuring ancient copper mines, stunning rock formations, and a beautiful lake.

The Eilat Promenade is a vibrant and lively area, lined with shops, restaurants, and bars, and offering stunning views of the Red Sea.

The Coral Beach Nature Reserve is a protected area, featuring an incredible array of coral and marine life, and offering snorkeling and diving opportunities.
