Tel Aviv: A Vibrant City

Tel Aviv is a city located on the Mediterranean coast of Israel, known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, and rich cultural scene.

Images

Attractions

Tel Aviv Beach

Enjoy the sun, sand, and sea at one of Tel Aviv's many beautiful beaches.

Carmel Market

Explore the vibrant stalls and shops of Carmel Market, offering a wide range of fresh produce, clothing, and souvenirs.

Tel Aviv Museum of Art

Discover an extensive collection of modern and contemporary art from around the world at the Tel Aviv Museum of Art.

Old Jaffa

Visit the historic neighborhood of Old Jaffa, with its narrow streets, charming shops, and picturesque views of the Mediterranean.

Rabin Square

Attend a concert or event, or simply relax in the large public space of Rabin Square, located in the heart of the city.
