Tel Aviv

Tel Aviv is a vibrant city located on the Mediterranean coast of Israel, known for its beautiful beaches, rich history, and thriving cultural scene.

Attractions

Tel Aviv Museum of Art

A modern art museum featuring a collection of Israeli and international art.

Carmel Market

A bustling marketplace filled with fresh produce, spices, and souvenirs.

White City

A UNESCO World Heritage Site featuring a collection of Bauhaus-style buildings.

Jaffa Old City

A historic neighborhood filled with narrow streets, charming shops, and picturesque views.

Palmach Museum

A museum showcasing the history of the Palmach, a Jewish military organization.
