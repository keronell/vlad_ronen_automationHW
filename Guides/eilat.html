Eilat: A City of Sun and Fun

Eilat is a beautiful city located on the Red Sea, known for its stunning beaches, vibrant nightlife, and rich history.

Recommended Attractions

Dolphin Reef

A unique opportunity to swim with dolphins in their natural habitat.

Underwater Observatory

Explore the underwater world of the Red Sea without getting wet.

Timna Park

A beautiful park featuring stunning natural landscapes and ancient copper mines.

Eilat Promenade

A vibrant waterfront area with shops, restaurants, and stunning views of the Red Sea.

Coral Beach Nature Reserve

A protected area featuring a stunning coral reef and diverse marine life.
