Florida: The Sunshine State

Florida is a state located in the southeastern United States, known for its beautiful beaches, theme parks, and vibrant cities.

Images

Attractions

Walt Disney World

A world-famous theme park resort located in Orlando, featuring four theme parks and two water parks.

Universal Orlando Resort

A popular theme park resort located in Orlando, featuring two theme parks, a water park, and several on-site hotels.

Everglades National Park

A unique and diverse ecosystem located in southern Florida, featuring mangrove forests, sawgrass marshes, and a wide variety of wildlife.

Kennedy Space Center Visitor Complex

A museum and educational center located on Merritt Island, showcasing the history of space exploration and the US space program.

Key West

A charming island city located at the southernmost tip of Florida, known for its beautiful beaches, historic architecture, and vibrant arts scene.
