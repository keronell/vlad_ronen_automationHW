Rome: The Eternal City

Rome, the capital of Italy, is a city steeped in history, art, and architecture. From ancient ruins to world-class museums, Rome has something to offer for every interest and taste.

Images of Rome

Attractions in Rome

The Colosseum is an iconic symbol of Rome, a massive amphitheater that once hosted gladiator battles and public events.

The Vatican City, an independent city-state within Rome, is home to numerous iconic landmarks, including St. Peter's Basilica and the Sistine Chapel.

The Pantheon, a magnificently preserved ancient temple, is a must-visit for history buffs and architecture enthusiasts.

The Roman Forum, a sprawling archaeological site, features a collection of ancient ruins, including temples, basilicas, and markets.

The Trevi Fountain, a beautiful baroque fountain, is a popular spot for tourists to make a wish and take in the city's vibrant atmosphere.
