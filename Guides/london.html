London: A Global City

London is a world-class city with a rich history, cultural landmarks, and modern attractions. From iconic skyscrapers to historic palaces, London has something to offer for everyone.

Recommended Attractions:

  • The British Museum: One of the world's greatest museums, with a collection of over 8 million objects.
  • The London Eye: A giant Ferris wheel offering stunning views of the city.
  • Buckingham Palace: The official residence of the British monarch, open to the public for tours.
  • The National Gallery: A world-class art museum featuring works by artists such as Van Gogh and Monet.
  • The Tower of London: A historic fortress and former prison, home to the Crown Jewels.
