London City

London is the capital and largest city of England, known for its rich history, cultural landmarks, and vibrant atmosphere.

Recommended Attractions

Buckingham Palace

The official residence of the British monarch, Buckingham Palace is a must-visit attraction in London.

The British Museum

One of the world's greatest museums, the British Museum houses a vast collection of artifacts from ancient civilizations.

The London Eye

For a panoramic view of the city, take a ride on the London Eye, a giant Ferris wheel located on the South Bank of the Thames.

The National Gallery

Featuring a world-class collection of Western European art, the National Gallery is a must-visit for art lovers.

Hyde Park

One of London's largest parks, Hyde Park offers boating, cycling, and walking opportunities, as well as plenty of green space to relax.
